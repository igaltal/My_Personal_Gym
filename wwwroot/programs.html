Training Programs

Discover our specialized training programs designed to help you achieve your fitness goals:

Beginner's Strength Training
A comprehensive 12-week program designed for beginners who want to build strength and muscle. This program focuses on the fundamental compound movements with progressive overload.
Duration: 12 weeks
Frequency: 3 times per week
Difficulty: Beginner
Focus: Strength building, muscle development
Weight Loss Challenge
An 8-week high-intensity program combining cardio and resistance training to maximize calorie burn and fat loss. Includes nutritional guidance and weekly check-ins.
Duration: 8 weeks
Frequency: 4-5 times per week
Difficulty: Intermediate
Focus: Fat loss, cardiovascular health
Advanced Powerlifting
A specialized 16-week program for experienced lifters looking to improve their strength in the squat, bench press, and deadlift. Includes periodization and peaking phases.
Duration: 16 weeks
Frequency: 4 times per week
Difficulty: Advanced
Focus: Strength, powerlifting competition
Functional Fitness
A 10-week program focusing on movement patterns that translate to everyday activities. Improves mobility, stability, and overall functional fitness.
Duration: 10 weeks
Frequency: 3 times per week
Difficulty: All levels
Focus: Mobility, functional strength
